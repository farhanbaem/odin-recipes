Taco in a Bag Recipe

Description

I love these walking taco bags! The chip bag serves as a shell for the tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 2 (2.5 ounce) packages corn chips
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 chopped fresh tomato
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup salsa
