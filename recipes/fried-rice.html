Fried rice is rice that is fried

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, chilled rice (white or brown)
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (carrots, onions, green onions, peas)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce (reduce if using regular soy sauce)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Rice
    Ensure your rice is thoroughly chilled before starting. Freshly cooked rice may turn mushy.
  2. Cook the Eggs
    Heat 1 tbsp butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    Pour in the beaten eggs and cook, stirring constantly, until scrambled. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  3. Cook the Veggies
    In the same pan, melt the remaining 1 tbsp butter.
    Add onions, carrots, and garlic. Saut√© for 3-4 minutes until softened.
    Add peas and green onions, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the Rice
    Increase heat to high and add your chilled rice. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  5. Season the Rice
    Add the cooked eggs back in.
    Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce, stirring to coat everything evenly.
  6. Final Touch
    Drizzle with toasted sesame oil for that rich, nutty flavor. Stir well.
  7. Serve
    Garnish with extra green onions if desired and serve hot!
